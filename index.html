Tabletop Roleplaying Game

Dungeons and Dragons is a type of tabletop roleplaying game where players take on the role of main characters within

a game put on by a game master. In this game, the players and game master work cooperatively to tell a story that the

game master offers to them, in the scope of actions taken by the player's characters. In this way, it is also a literary

device that creates a story over the course of play.



What Happens?

In the game, the game master is constantly generating content for the players and their characters to interact with, whether

that be through other characters to speak with, puzzles that the players need to figure out the answer to, or combat, which

is possible with rules within this game that allow the characters to combat other threats within the game as a team.
