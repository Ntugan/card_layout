Example Adventures

There are many prewritten adventure modules that a game master can use to run with the players! Here are some examples.

Storm King's Thunder

2016

This adventure has the players placed in the middle of the politics of giants, trying to find a way to stop a war that the giants plan to wage.

Waterdeep Dragon Heist

2018

A famous explorer approaches the players, asking them to complete a quest for him. This begins a mad romp through the massive cities districts as the players uncover villains plots!

Tomb of Annihilation

2017

The players find out that a magical disease is spreading known as the death curse. Intelligence has discovered the cause of this located on a dangerous jungle island, riddled with culture and traps. Go in and put a stop to this curse!

Hoard of the Dragon Queen

2014

In this adventure, on the coast that the players are from, a cult is trying to bring about bring back the queen of dragons, Tiamat, from her prison. To this end, the cult razes the countryside in search of money and powerful objects to offer to the queen. The players are asked to help stop these cultists!

Rise of Tiamat

2014

A continuation from the adventure Hoard of the Dragon Queen, picking up where the previous left off. The cult that aims to bring back their imprisoned dragon queen grows stronger by the day, with the battle becoming increasingly more political as tensions rise. What will the players do to help?
